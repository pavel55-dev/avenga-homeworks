Types of Investigations

  • Terrorism

    To counter terrorism, the FBI investigates threats and activities aimed at undermining national security and public safety.

  • Cybercrime

    The FBI investigates cybercrimes including hacking, identity theft, and online fraud to protect individuals and organizations from digital threats.

  • White-Collar Crime

    The FBI investigates white-collar crimes such as fraud, embezzlement, and corruption to maintain the integrity of financial and governmental institutions.

  • Violent Crime

    The FBI investigates violent crimes including homicides, kidnappings, and assaults to ensure public safety and uphold the rule of law.
